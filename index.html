Saint Mikael Church (Wukro)

Saint Mikael Church is a historic rock-hewn church in Wukro. Carved centuries ago, it has been a center of worship and pilgrimage for generations.

This church represents God’s mercy and protection over our family.

×

Medhane Alem Adi Kesho Church

Medhane Alem Adi Kesho is a rock-hewn church carved from solid rock, one of Tigray’s oldest and most remarkable churches.

It reminds our family of God’s grace, protection, and blessings.
